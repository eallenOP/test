Rural recycling facilities

Middlemarch Community Recycling Facility

  • This recycling facility is located in the car park at the back of the Kissing Gate Café at 5 South Road. Residents in the greater Strath Taieri area, currently not receiving the DCC’s kerbside collection service, are encouraged to use this recycling facility.


Lee Stream Community Recycling Facility

  • This recycling facility is located behind Lee Stream Community Hall, next to Lee Stream School on Clarks Junction-Lee Stream Road (coming from Outram, just before George King Memorial Drive).


Hoopers Inlet Community Recycling Facility

  • This recycling facility is located at the entrance of the Hoopers Inlet Hall on Allans Beach Road, Cape Saunders. Coming from Portobello, continue on Allans Beach Road turning left once at Hoopers Inlet. Hoopers Inlet Hall is approximately 100 metres on the left.

Transfer Stations​


Transfer stations accept general waste for landfill disposal. All general waste is transported to Victoria Flats landfill in the Gibbston Valley.​​ Fees are charged for disposal of general waste, the fees cover operating costs, landfill costs and a contribution towards waste minimisation programme costs.


Different fees apply depending on the type and separation of material. If the material can be diverted from landfill it generally has a lesser fee as the costs associated with it are less. It pays to separate material for reuse and recycling!​​
