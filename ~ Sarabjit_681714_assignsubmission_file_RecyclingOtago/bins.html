DCC rubbish bags


Where you can get DCC rubbish bags

DCC rubbish bags can be bought from DCC Customer Service Centres and Agencies, Otago Polytechnic and Otago University Students Associations, supermarkets and some dairies and petrol stations.


What can't you put in your DCC rubbish bags?

We only collect general household waste. This is to keep our rubbish collectors safe and also part of our ongoing effort to reduce waste going to landfill.


We won't collect your rubbish if it contains:

  • Green waste & soil, hot ashes, liquids and paint
  • Unwrapped broken glass or sharp objects
  • Medical waste (needles and syringes, please contact your nearest pharmacy for free, safe disposal, unwanted/expired medication, please contact your nearest pharmacy for free, safe disposal) and hazardous waste
  • Loose ashes - ashes must be cold and secured or wrapped and placed in the rubbish bag

Can you fill your DCC rubbish bag to capacity?

DCC rubbish bags are designed to hold up to 15 kg of weight. Anything more than this becomes a health and safety issue for our contractors and they won't take them away.

Replacement yellow-lidded bins

The bins are available in two different sizes, 240 Litre and 80 Litre. If your bin is lost, or broken, or you wish to change the size of bin contact the property owner to replace it or contact DCC to arrange delivery of a replacement. There is a delivery charge of $30.
