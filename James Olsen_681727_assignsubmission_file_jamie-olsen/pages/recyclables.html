Recyclables

Listed below are items which are considered recyclables and what colour bins to put them in.

Yellow-lidded Recycling Bin

These below items can go inside a yellow-lidded recycling bin:

  • Rigid plastic packaging containers with lids on, such as milk & water bottles.
  • Tins & cans
  • Aluminium trays and foil
  • Flattened cardboard
  • Paper
  • Egg cartons

It is reccomended that you rinse plastics, tins and cans.

Glass Recycling Bin

These below items can go inside a glass recycling bin:

  • Glass bottles
  • Glass jars

E-Waste

In Dunedin you are able to hand in your e-waste to any OfficeMax retail store, via a service called TechCollect.

Accepted items are:

  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops
  • Tablets
  • Monitors
  • Printers
  • Peripherals
  • Cameras
