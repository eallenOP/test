What to Recycle?

When recycling there are commonly three typical ways to recycle, blue, yellow and red bins. There is also a paid DCC bag that can be used for common household rubbish and food scraps.

Blue Bin

The blue bins are primarily used to recycle glass bottles and jars. When recycling glass it should be unbroken and any residue must be rinsed out.

Red Bin

The red bin is the same as the DCC bags, it can hold any food scraps and general house rubbish. In order to get a red bin you will need to order a the bin from waste management, it comes in three different sizes 80L, 140L and 240L.

Yellow Bin

The yellow bins can recylce cetain items such as:

Plastic
  • Soft drinks
  • Water bottles
  • Biscuit trays
  • Detergent bottles
  • Fruit junce bottles
  • Milk bottles
  • Ice cream containers
  • Margarine containers
  • Liquid soap containers
Paper and cardboard
  • Newspaper
  • letters
  • Magazines
  • Egg trays
  • Wrapping paper
  • Toilet roll inners
  • Flattened boxs and cartons
Cans and Tins
  • Aluminium tins and cans
  • Steel tins and cans
  • Aluminium trays and foil
  • Metal pie trays
  • Foil
  • Aerosol cans
