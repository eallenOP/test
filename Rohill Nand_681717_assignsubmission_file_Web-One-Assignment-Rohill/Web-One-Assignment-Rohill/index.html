How to be eco-friendly

Changing to a more eco friendly lifestyle will help in keeping the environment clean and tidy also providing a healthier and efficient life choices.

This section will list out specific examples that you can either impelement in your own life or have further knowledge for future awareness.

Cooking Wise

  • Use cloth napkins instead of paper towels.
  • Use a presure cooker
  • Cook with residual heat
  • Eat less meat
  • Grow your own fruit and veges
  • Start a compost pile

Water Wise

  • Start using reusable water bottles
  • Install a low flow showerhead
  • Turn off your tap inbetween brushing teeth or washing you face
  • Repair any leaking taps
  • Take shorter showers

Energy Wise

  • Change to an energy efficient light bulb
  • Turn down the water heater
  • Get insulation installed
  • Carpool to minimize carbon
  • Consider using solar and an alternative source of power
  • Buy energy-efficient appliances

Object Wise

  • Recycling and get some money back
  • Lighted the load in your car to use less fuel
  • Borrow from neighbours and friends, dont buy
  • Make you own cleaning supplies
  • Try making your own DIY products
