Recycling In Otago




WHAT'S THE IMPORTANCE?


  1. Product waste have significant impacts on climate, ecosystems, water, air and communities.

  2. Plastic is a major form of pollution worldwide. These can harm marine life & may affect life higher on the food chain including humans.

  3. Food waste makes a significant contribution to the waste sector's greenhouse gas emissions.

  4. Landfills cost millions to develop and often the sites can't be used for other purposes for decades - they produce methane which is a potent greenhouse gas.


“We have significant problems in NZ where, according to the Ministry for the Environment, we send 2.5 million tonnes of waste to landfills each year. We can do a lot better when we compare ourselves to similar countries. Recycling effectively is a critical way we can reduce this amount of waste going to landfills."
