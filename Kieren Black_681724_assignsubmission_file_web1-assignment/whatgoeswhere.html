What goes where?

You will see many of these around the city. They are for recycling. They have seperate areas for glass, plastic and general waste.

For general household waste there are kerbside collections that will take away most waste. For large volumes or hazardous waste, you can take this to your local landfill or transfer station.

This is the yellow bin for general household waste. These are colleted from your kerbside weekly.

Please do not put the following into the Yellow Bin

  • Garden waste
  • Plastic bags and soft plastics
  • Textiles, clothing, shoes
  • All milk and juice cartons, waxy paper and cardboard, soiled paper (e.g. with food), juice/tomato sauce/stock boxes and cartons.
  • Medical Waste
  • Hazardous items
  • Expanded Polystyrene
