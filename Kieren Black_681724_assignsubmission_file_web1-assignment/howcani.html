How can I be Enviromentally Friendly?

Steps to become more Enviromentally Friendly

  • Use a reusable cups and straws
  • Op shop/ buy second hand
  • Check a brands ethical status
  • Reusable bags aren't just for groceries!
  • Ditch the plastic wrap!
  • Conserve Water
  • Use Less Fossil Fuel Based Products
  • Reduce Use of Harmful Chemicals
  • Composting
  • Buy Recycled Products
  • Stop Littering
