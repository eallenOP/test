Welcome

Recycle Otago is a Website that is a website dedicated to helping with education surrounding recycling within the Greater Otago region! Contained within site is information regarding what you can and can't recycling throughout, Dunedin Balclutha, Queenstown, and Central Otago. It also provides information on how to dispose properly of your electronic e-waste materials. We've even compiled together a fun quiz for you to test your knowledge about what you have learned!
