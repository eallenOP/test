E-Waste

What is E-waste

E-waste is unwanted or broken electronics. This is not limited to appliances such as Tv's or computers but includes whiteware aswell.

Should i dispose of E-waste in the landfill?

>No some of the components can be hazardous to the environment. Hazardous chemicals in batteries for example, can leach out into the soil which can contaminate drinking water.

Resell or donate

If you have unwanted electronics consider selling or donating them instead of throwing them away. This helps keep hazardous materials out of landfills and maybe even net you a little bit of money :)

Recycleling centres

If your electronics are broken, then instead of throwing them in the landfill take them to a resource recovery center. E-waste can contain precious resources that can be easily recycled and used for other things.
