Reduce your foot print

Want to reduce you impact on the enviroment? Here are some tips

Reduse

Iinstead of using single-use items swap to long term replacement. Instead of getting a disposable cup with your coffee bring your own reusable one

Reuse

Check your local Landfill and opportunity shops they often resell useful goods at very cheap prices. The Green Island Landfill in Dunedin resells a wide range of items, ranging from clothes to a new bed.

Recycle

Don't throw everything into the waste bin. Be sure to properly recycle, if you need help learning how to recycle you can find out here

Save Energy

Try to save energy where you can. Electronics on standby still use power. Switch them off at the wall to properly save energy.

Save Water

Water is precious and you should try to save it. Stop taking lengthy showers. When brushing your teeth remember to turn off the taps while brushing.

Save Petrol

If you can try to carpool with people traveling to the same destination as you. This will prevent gas emissions. If you cant carpool why not try biking instead?
